Eclipses

Eclipses are celestial events that have fascinated humans for millennia. They occur when one astronomical body partially or fully obscures the light from another celestial body.

These events happen due to the intricate dance of the Sun, Moon, and Earth in space, creating awe-inspiring visual spectacles that have influenced cultures, myths, and scientific understanding throughout history.

Solar and Lunar Eclipses

Solar Eclipse

A solar eclipse occurs when the Moon passes between Earth and the Sun, thereby totally or partly obscuring the image of the Sun for a viewer on Earth.

Lunar Eclipse

A lunar eclipse occurs when the Earth comes between the Sun and the Moon, causing the Earth's shadow to fall on the Moon.
