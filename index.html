Cosmic Eclipses

Eclipses are celestial events that have fascinated humans for millennia. These cosmic phenomena occur when one astronomical body partially or fully obscures the light from another celestial body.

Throughout history, these celestial events have shaped cultures, inspired myths, and advanced our scientific understanding of the cosmos.

Solar Eclipse

A solar eclipse occurs when the Moon passes between Earth and the Sun, creating one of nature's most awe-inspiring spectacles. During a total solar eclipse, the Moon completely blocks the Sun's bright face, revealing the solar corona in all its glory.

Lunar Eclipse

During a lunar eclipse, Earth passes between the Sun and Moon, casting its shadow on the lunar surface. This celestial alignment can turn the Moon a dramatic red color, known as a 'Blood Moon', creating a spectacular night sky display.
